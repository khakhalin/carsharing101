# Appendix: Default values and parameters

Call center: about 10€ per call:
* assuming a salary of 30k a year, 260 working days a year, 20 days of vacation, an 8-hour working day, and 10 minutes per call, with an unachievable perfect load, we are ending up with about 3 €/call in salary alone. 
* Let's assume that we need to have 2 times more call agents (Erlang formula with 10 min waiting time and 30% shrinkage), which gives us 6€/call in salary.
* Renting one office space (let's assume 1k€ per month) will add 2€/call more, resulting in 8€/call.
* We'll also need to lease all sorts of technical infrastructure, arriving at about 10-15 €/call.
